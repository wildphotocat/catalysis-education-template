# Catalysis Education Newsletter - Article Submission Template

Official LaTeX template for submitting articles to the Catalysis Education Newsletter.

## About

This template is provided by the National Centre for Catalysis Research (NCCR) Alumni at Indian Institute of Technology Madras.

## How to Use

1. Download `article_template.tex`
2. Open it in your LaTeX editor (TeXstudio, Overleaf, etc.)
3. Fill in your article metadata (title, author, dates, etc.)
4. Write your content
5. Compile with PDFLaTeX or XeLaTeX
6. Submit to catalysiseducation@gmail.com

## Requirements

- LaTeX distribution (TeX Live, MiKTeX, or MacTeX)
- Required packages: newtxtext, newtxmath, mathpazo, hyperref, etc. (all standard)

## Submission Guidelines

- Email submissions to: catalysiseducation@gmail.com
- Include all figures separately
- Provide complete citations with DOI links
- Acknowledge any use of AI tools
